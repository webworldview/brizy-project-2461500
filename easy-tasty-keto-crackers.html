Keto Cheese Crackers


Introduction

There is nothing better than having a nice bowl of keto snacks with you when you get that craving for food. The trouble is most of the snacks you get from the local store tend to be full of carbs and not healthy for anyone on the Keto Diet.

Containing Almond flour and only


This is the ideal recipe for snack junkies who just need one more snack before dinner, or for those just wanting a nice nibble while sitting down with some guests. Of course the main thing is that they are Keto and these most certainly are.


They are simple to whip up and taste delicious.. just ask anyone who has had one.

Ingredients

2 x Cups (6oz/170g) shredded mozzarella cheese

1 x Cup (4oz/115g) almond flour

¼ x Cup (2oz/57g) cream cheese1 x egg

2 x Tbsp mixed seeds

1 x Tbsp of sesame

1 x Tbsp of caraway

What to do

Before you start - Preheat the Oven to 425ºF (210ºC)

First put the cheese and cream cheese in a bowl, and pour the almond flour over the top. Being sure to cover the cheese with the almond flour.

Then Cover the bowl, with a paper towel or cloth, and microwave on high for about 30-60 seconds (make sure the cheese is melted)

After taking out the mixture, stir it thoroughly with a spatula. Mixing the cheese and Almond flour until a dough forms.

Allow the mixture to cool until it is warm to touch, then add in the egg. Mixing it quickly and thoroughly into a ball.

Now that you have a ball of dough, put the dough between two large pieces of baking paper and use a rolling pin to flatten till its around 1/8 of an inch thick.

Then peel back the parchment paper, and transfer the now flattened dough mixture to a greased baking tray, and sprinkle with seeds.

Bake the crackers in the oven for 8-9 minutes or until golden brown and crisp.

To finish the crackers, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool for 1-2 minutes before cutting them with a pizza cutter or sharp knife!
