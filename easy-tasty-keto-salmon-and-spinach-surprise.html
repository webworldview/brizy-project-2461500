Salmon and Spinach Surprise

by Donna Johnisee

Introduction

PLAYING "Creative Chef" in my Kitchen..... Do YOU ever just " Throw ingredients together"?

Ingredients

1 x TBsp Unsalted Butter,

2 x TBsp fresh Onions

2 x TBsp Celery Minced

1 x Cup of Frozen Spinach (Or 2 x Cups Fresh Spinach)

8 x tsp Himalayan Pink Salt

3 x Whipped Eggs (Scrambled)

1 x Tbsp Heavy Cream

5oz x Salmon

1 x TBsp MCT Oil

What to do

Put the Butter, Onions, Celery, and Himalayan PINK Salt, and Sauté until tender.

Then add the 1 Cup of frozen - thawed spinach, squeezed dry,

(or 2 cups of Fresh) and Warm in the Pan

After that add the 3 Whipped Eggs (Scrambled) with the 1 tablespoon of Heavy Cream to the ingredients in pan and Cook.

Finally, Move the mixture to the sides of the pan and make an opening in the centre. Drop in and warm up the 5oz of Salmon before Serving.

Tip: You can top with MCT Oil, and Salt & Pepper to taste.
